Blockchain Donations

Exploring fast, transparent ways to support refugee engineers and projects.

Why blockchain?
Traditional funding routes for refugees and displaced professionals can be slow, expensive, and opaque. By using blockchain, Iskandar is enthusiastic about building support channels that are:

  • Borderless – sending funds directly to engineers and community projects.
  • Transparent – anyone can verify how much was sent, where, and when.
  • Composable – easily integrated with training platforms and remote work systems.

The goal is not speculation, but stability and dignity: ensuring that refugee professionals and evacuees can get paid reliably for their work and receive support for training, equipment, and project costs.

Example donation flow (concept)
  • 1. Choose a blockchain network with low fees and good tooling.
  • 2. Send funds to a public wallet controlled by a trusted project team.
  • 3. Track all incoming funds and outgoing payments on-chain.
  • 4. Route payments to Syrian engineers, evacuees, and training partners.
  • 5. Publish periodic impact reports linking transactions to real outcomes.
